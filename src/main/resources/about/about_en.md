# About Handicap Committee App

This application helps golf club handicap committee members verify scorecard submissions quickly and consistently.

## How it uses Golf Canada

- It signs in using your Golf Canada account.
- It looks up member profiles and recent playing history from Golf Canada.
- It compares uploaded scorecard dates with Golf Canada rounds to highlight matches and possible mismatches.

## Why this app exists

The goal is to support committee review decisions with reliable data while keeping the verification workflow simple.
